Exploring Climate Change Impacts on Soragune's Agriculture

This project entails an in-depth investigation into the intersection of climate dynamics and the agricultural practices of the community in Soragune, located in Haldummulla. Through field visits and engaging conversations with villagers engaged in various professions, including farming, business, and others, the project aimed to comprehend the challenges and successes experienced by the community in response to climate change. The report will meticulously document these findings, highlighting the severe effects of erratic weather patterns on agricultural output and the limitations imposed by outdated farming techniques. With a focus on disseminating relevant information and offering recommendations for sustainable farming practices, the project seeks to empower the resilient people of Soragune to adapt to changing climatic conditions effectively.
